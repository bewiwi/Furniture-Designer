# Manufacturing

Once your 3D design is complete, you can move onto the manufacturing phase.

## Cut List
The **Cut List** tab provides a flat list of every individual wooden plank you need to purchase or cut. It properly accounts for the global wood thickness parameter specified in the designer.

## Cut Plan (Packer)
The **Cut Plan** tab takes your Cut List and packs it optimally into standard commercial panel dimensions (e.g., 2440x1220mm MDF or Plywood sheets). This ensures minimum material waste.
