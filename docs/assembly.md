# Assembly & Tools

The software supports an innovative dowel-based assembly approach. It automatically computes the exact positions for wooden dowels along the edges and faces of every colliding plank.

## 3D Printable Jigs
To physically drill the holes efficiently, you don't need a CNC. The **Tools** tab dynamically generates custom 3D models for two jigs:
1. **Edge Guide:** Drills into the thickness (kerf) of boards.
2. **Face Guide:** Drills perpendicularly into the flat face of boards.

You can download these as `.stl` files and 3D print them!
