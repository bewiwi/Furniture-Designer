# 3D Design

The **Furniture Designer** centers around a recursive space-partitioning design paradigm. Instead of drawing individual boards, you allocate functional space, and the geometry solves itself.

## Creating Compartments
By clicking on any 3D node, you can split it into:
- **Rows**: Horizontal dividers.
- **Columns**: Vertical dividers.

You can interactively resize each compartment, and lock it to a precise dimension to ensure it perfectly fits your items (e.g. your TV, receiver, or storage boxes).
